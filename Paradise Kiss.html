YUKARI

Yukari Hayasaka was once a high school student, but she later becomes a model for the Treetop agency.

GEORGE

Jōji Koizumi, better known as George, is a fashion designer and a student at Yazawa School for the Arts. Also the love interest of Yukari Hayasaka

MIWAKO

Miwako Sakurada is a former student of Yazawa School for the Arts who currently works for Mikako Kouda (Miwako's sister) at Happy Berry and the girlfriend/later wife of Arashi Nagase.

ARASHI

Arashi is the son of Takeshi Nagase (a rock star) and Risa Kanzaki (a housewife) who is also a childhood friend and love interest of Miwako Sakurada and Hiroyuki Tokumori.

ISABELLA

Isabella Yamamoto is a transgender woman who acts as a mother figure to the members of Paradise Kiss. She is also good friends with Jōji Koizumi and she is inspired by his very first designs.
